# Blender
This is the first time making foliage in blender so I had to find some tutorials on how to make foliage, I knew that I wanted a low poly art style for my game so I searched up low poly foliage on YouTube and google and found these two tutorials below 
![https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a2vhQ6GfXIw&t=1s](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a2vhQ6GfXIw&t=1s)
This tutorial goes over how to make several foliage and nature elements like trees, rocks, grass, and etc. The video showed me several tricks on how to make low poly foliage for example to make a low poly bush in blender an easy way is to create an isometric sphere and set the subdivisions to one this makes a bush like shape and by duplicating, scaling, and moving the sphere next to one another it can make it look more like a bush with variety.
Another trick on how to make grass in blender is actually quite simple, the You tuber made a cube scaled it down and shaped it into a rectangle, after he extruded up and moved the new face at a slight angle, and once again he did the same but merged the face to make the sharp point grass has. After making a single blade of grass he created a few more variations and then put them together to make a patch of grass in blender. 

The video above shows how to make every foliage type that I will need however I actually found this video before the above video, I also prefer this style over the one in the video above 
![https://youtu.be/hvxoAX_poI0](https://youtu.be/hvxoAX_poI0)
this tutorial is very simple however it is very short so many people may find it hard to understand especially for beginners, however I have experience with blender so I was able to understand it very well. The tutorial starts by creating a cube and merging it at centre then by using the extrude tool to make a trunk and branches, then to add tickness to the tree using the skin modifier. Then to add leaves by using a similar tick to the video above with an isometric sphere and setting the subdivisions to one and using them as leaves.
