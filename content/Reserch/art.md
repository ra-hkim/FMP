The art style of my game is unique my characters will be made from 3D shapes with cel shading to make the game more visually appealing and to create a unique, distinctive look and to break away from UE5s default look. There are several games and peaces of media that have inspired the art style of my game

### Splatoon series

The Splatoon series is a 3rd person shooter game using ink as ammunition allowing teams to paint the map in bright colors.

The games art style, tone, and settings are bright and saturated, using neon colors on everything that the player sees as well as even brighter colors for inks and character hair. The character design is quirky, childish, and improper body proportions, the world feels bright and energy filled with its urban layout every aspect radiates a bright and fun personality.

The games art style is very effective, the bright saturated neon colors dominate everything instantly getting the players attention and its not just visual as the colors serves a purpose for gameplay by using the inks colors to represent areas that each team has captured. The cute character designs make the player feel attached towards their character.

the art style is unique but I wont be copying it word for word I want to steal the bright colors that Splatoon uses many people find it very appealing and for a shooter game it is unique compared to many popular titles like rainbow 6, I want to take the games bright color palate and use it in mine to create a unique visually appealing game and to create similar feelings that Splatoon dose.

### Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open world RPG game set in the world of Teyvat in attempt to save the characters sibling, the game has an extensive and long story which is something that I enjoyed while playing it myself, alongside an extensive combat system and a gacha system allowing people to unlock an extent of characters.

The art style is unique however it keeps consistent alongside other Hoyoverse games, however compared to Honkai impact 3rd which takes on a scifi style Genshin impact takes on a fantasy style, both game use cel shading and outlines to create an anime style game. However the colors are pretty different Genshin Impact uses warm natural colors, character's clothes have an older style one example is Layla her clothes are made from deep blue and white fabrics alongside gold accents which support the games fantastic style.

The games art style is very effective the art style as Hoyoverse target stylized over realism the art is simple it uses soft cel shading to create a simple yet very effective art style, the artists also creates clear character silhouettes to make characters easily recognizable allowing players to recognize a character from their silhouette alone, the artists have used distinct hair shapes, asymmetrical clothing, and unique recognizable accessories, all of this combined  makes every character's design very effective. Another feature that makes the games art style extremely effective is the strong use of color identity by making features of characters different colors depending on what element each character controls for example: 

Anemo (wind) = turquoise

Pyro (fire) = red and orange

Electro (electricity) = purple

Genshin impact has several aspects of its art style that makes it very unique and effective however I cant copy their amazing art style so I will be borrowing a few things from them, I am planning to take the use of easily recognizable shapes making my enemies and player have different distinct shapes allowing them to be recognized from a distance, I also want to use color identity to also make characters more identifiable by players.
