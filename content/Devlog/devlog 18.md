## Landscape Material
I also made a landscape material using simple colours connected to a landscape layer blend allowing the landscape to blend between the different colours smoothly it also means different areas of the landscape can have different colours/materials.
![[Screenshot 2026-05-05 091523.png]]
I added 4 layers to the blend with 3 shades of green and a black colour to add dark patches in my landscape. After making the material I applied it to the landscape and went into the landscape editor paint tool, from the paint tool I used the automatic layer function to find all the layers and create weight blends for them which simply allow the colours to be blended together. 
After this I painted different sections with the colours I added in my material
