## Out of CTRL
This game takes a simple puzzle game concept and makes it significantly more complicated instead of just trying to escape a puzzle map the player has to move keys level, these keys relate to the actual keys on the keyboard meaning that the player has to move the keys on the map to change the key bindings of the game, you can see in the image that the CTRL key is not inside the box labelled "Press to continue" this means that the player cant press CTRL to continue so they have to use the character to move the player into the respective box,

## Rounds
Rounds is a game that I personally own and have played with a lot  friend and have enjoyed quite a bit, The main goal of the game is to defeat the opposing player, you do this with a gun to shoot the other play and a shield to block bullets, however the main concept is the use of cards to effect game-play you you can see below an image of the games card picker it allows you to pick form several cards that can change many game-play aspects. In the example below the player has is highlighting "FROST SLAM" this creates a few effects that can change how both you and the other player fight, firstly the main effect of the card is to slow the other player when they are in proximity of you but only when you use the block move by right clicking. Alongside slowing the opponent down it also gives the player more HP and unfortunately increases the block cool-down.
